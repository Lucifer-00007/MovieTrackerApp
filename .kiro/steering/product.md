# Product Overview

MovieTracker is a cross-platform mobile application for discovering and tracking movies, TV series, and anime content worldwide.

## Core Purpose
- Surface trending movies, web-series, and anime globally
- Provide region-specific "best of" lists (Hollywood, Bollywood, Anime, Chinese, Russian, Spanish, German, etc.)
- Enable users to search, save, and track content with offline support

## Target Users
- Casual viewers looking for trending content
- Binge-watchers who queue and download for offline viewing
- Genre specialists (anime fans, documentary enthusiasts)
- International viewers seeking localized content and subtitles

## Key Features (MVP)
- Global trending feed with carousels and ranked lists
- Country hubs with regional top content
- Movie/series detail pages (synopsis, cast, trailers, ratings)
- Search with filters (country, genre, year)
- Watchlist and favorites
- Offline downloads
- Light/dark theme support
- Localization and region switching
